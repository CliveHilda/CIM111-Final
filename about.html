About Us


  • Background
  • Our Mission
  • Our Plans


Kinross Sexual Abuse Prevention (KSAP) was started on April 20, 2019 after KSAP's founder and CEO, Talia Kinross, was touched by the stories of victims of sexual assault and vowed to do as much as possible to aid people like them and lessen the number of people like them.

Kinross leads a talented team of experts from the fields of victim services, communications, technology, and public policy. Together with the board of directors, they’ve helped KSAP become the nation’s most recent anti-sexual violence organization.

Our mission is to to fight against causes and impacts of sexual abuse in the United States and to extend support to anyone and everyone who needs it.

As ambitious as it may sound, we hope to see a day when we're able to close our doors and shut down completely because we're no longer needed.

We hope to expand as much as possible to cover all 50 states as well as all U.S. territories. Eventually, we also seek to go international with our services.

We are looking to expand our services to help victims of domestic violence as well.

With enough fundraising, we will create our very own Formula 1 team to race in the name of victims of sexual assault everywhere.

If you would like to donate and support us in our goals, please go to our contact page.
